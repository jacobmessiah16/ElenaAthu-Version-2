Meet Elena — Where Education Gets Real

In under 2 minutes, Elena tells you exactly why she started this site, what she teaches, and why it’s nothing like school.

FAQ

What topics do you cover?

Anything school forgot to teach us — personal finance, weird history, survival basics, mind-blowing “why didn’t they tell us this” facts, and spicy life skills.

Where do you get your information from?

Books, credible sources, obsessively deep dives, and sometimes ADHD-fueled 3am rabbit holes. I double-check facts.

Why are your videos so chaotic?

Because life is chaotic. And so is learning. If you wanted a traditional lecture, go back to school. I’m here to make your brain go ✨“wait… WHAT?”✨

Will you ever do online courses or private lessons?

Nah. That sounds like scheduling and responsibility, which I’m allergic to. My content is for the masses — not the inbox.

How can I support you?

Watch the videos. Like, share, yell about them to your group chat. Bonus points if you quote me at a dinner party and make someone uncomfortable.
